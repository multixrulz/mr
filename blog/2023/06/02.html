NASA is developing a robot probe to send to Enceladus

Enceladus is a moon of Saturn that is covered in ice. It is believed to have liquid water under the ice.

Unlike the rovers that have gone to our moon and Mars, this robot is designed to move around in an icy world and moves like a snake.

What grade letters mean

A

You know a lot about the topic and can easily use what you know.

You are very good at the skills and can use them in new situations.

B

You know a lot about the topic and can use what you know.

You are very good at the skills.

C

You know the main parts of the topic well.

You are okay at the skills.

D

You know some things about the topic.

You are starting to learn the skills.

E

You know a little about the topic.

You are just beginning to learn some of the skills.

Chimborazo

What is Chimborazo?

Chimborazo is an encyclopaedia game. We play it on Wikipedia.

The game begins with a Wikipedia page. You read the page until you find something else interesting to read about. Look up that new thing on Wikipedia. Keep going like this until you get back to the original Wikipedia page.

This TED talk explains it in more detail and gives an example.

How to play

Join the Google Classroom that has the code vxohznm. Contact Mr Henley to be added.

Each week there will be an “assignment”. The assignment will specify a starting entry on Wikipedia. You will then:

  1. Open the Google Doc for the assignment. It will already have the initial Wikipedia page on it.
  2. Read (some of) the page. Find something interesting, and look it up on Wikipedia. *. Save the link to the new page into the Google Doc.
  3. Read (some of) the new page. Find something interesting, and look it up on Wikipedia. *. Save the link to the new page into the Google Doc. Continue until you come back to your original page.

Submit your assignment containing links to the Wikipedia pages in your Chimborazo. Better entries will include a little bit of information, or a picture, etc.

When everyone has entered their Chimborazo, we will vote on the one you like the most - there will be a form to do that on.

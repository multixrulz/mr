Late assessment tasks - years 7-10

  • No marks lost
  • Students are ineligible for:
    • academic awards (e.g. 1st, 2nd, 3rd)
    • a commendation for the task
  • An illness/misadventure form may be used and family may be contacted to get the task submitted
  • There will be a “letter of concern” for years 7 to 9
  • The N warning process will be followed for Y10

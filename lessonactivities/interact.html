Behaviour for Learning

Interact

We are learning by listening to each other. My job is to coordinate the conversation.

  • Stay in your seat
  • Take part in the discussion
  • Don’t talk until you are invited to
  • Put up your hand if you need to say something
  • Look at the person who is talking
  • Listen carefully

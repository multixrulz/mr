Behaviour for Learning

Arduino

We are learning new skills by trying things we might not have done before.

  • Stay in your seat
  • Speak quietly to people next to you
  • Have a go at the Arduino practical
  • If you get stuck, ask:
    • Someone nearby
    • Me
